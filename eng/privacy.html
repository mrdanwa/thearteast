Privacy Policy

Updated in January, 2025

1. Introduction

At The Arteast Agency (hereinafter, "we", "our" or "the website"), we respect the privacy of our visitors and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

Our website only collects the following personal data:

  • Email: when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Name, email, and message: when you fill out the contact form.

We do not automatically collect any other type of personal information.

3. Use of Information

The provided data will be used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Sending the newsletter with relevant content if you have subscribed.
  • Responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form.

We do not share, sell, or transfer personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

The collected data will be stored for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Newsletter data will be retained as long as the visitor does not unsubscribe.
  • Messages from the contact form will be stored for as long as necessary to manage the inquiry.

5. Data Security

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alterations, or disclosures.

6. Visitor Rights

As a visitor, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to the use of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at in@thearteasteast.es.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be published on this page along with the update date.
